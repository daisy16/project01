Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is third of three portraints of the Riviere family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 X 70 cm
Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
